2. Strategy of using Editors or IDEs:
    1. Pycharm: Advantages: Good for pure python.
                Disadvantages: Not good at for Low-End Computers and Web Development in Django.

    2. Spyder: Advantages: Works good on Low-End Computer.
               Disadvantages: Not good for beginner because it is built top of python.

    3. Jupyter Notebook: Advantages: Good for running programs in pieces.
                         Disadvantages: Not good at Web Development.

    4. Jupyter Lab: Advantages: Good at Web Development
                    Disadvantages: Still in Development

    5. VS Code: Advantages: All-in-One IDE.
                Disadvantages: Not good for Data Science
